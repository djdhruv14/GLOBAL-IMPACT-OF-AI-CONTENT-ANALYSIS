This project leverages Tableau for advanced visual analytics and SQL for complex data aggregation to evaluate the intersection of technological adoption and economic shifts. Its core objective is to analyze how generative AI influences global revenue growth and job displacement across diverse industries, while simultaneously measuring the impact of regulatory frameworks on consumer trust and adoption. By mapping the efficiency of specific AI tools against macroeconomic outcomes, the project provides a strategic roadmap for identifying sectors that are effectively augmenting human labor versus those primarily focused on automation-driven cost reduction.
